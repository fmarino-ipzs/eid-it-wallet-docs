PID/(Q)EAA Issuance
===================

This section describes the PID and (Q)EAAs issuance flow with a high level of security.
The relevant entities and interfaces involved in the issuance flow are:

    - *Wallet Provider*,
    - *Wallet Solution*,
    - *Wallet Instance*,
    - *PID Provider*,
    - *National Identity Provider*,
    - *(Q)EAA Provider*,
    - *Authentic Source*.
